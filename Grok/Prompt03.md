**Shopify costs for a knife store (pocket knives, kitchen knives, swords, rifle scopes, etc.) typically range from $29–$105/month for the platform subscription on the most common plans, but the real total is higher when including payment processing, apps, domain, and other fees.** A realistic starting range for a small-to-medium operation is **$100–$500+/month** depending on sales volume, apps needed, and scale.

### Core Shopify Subscription Plans (2026)
These are the main options for a full online store:

- **Starter**: $5/month — Limited (social/media selling, no full store). Not suitable for a proper knife/gear shop.
- **Basic**: **$39/month** (billed monthly) or **$29/month** (billed annually, ~$348/year). Best starting point for most new stores.
- **Grow** (formerly Shopify plan): **$105/month** or **$79/month** annually. Better reports, lower fees, more staff accounts. Good as you scale.
- **Advanced**: **$399/month** or **$299/month** annually. For higher volume with advanced reporting/shipping.
- **Shopify Plus**: Starts at ~$2,300–$2,500/month. Enterprise level, not needed initially.

**Tip**: Start with Basic on annual billing for savings (up to 25%). Shopify often offers trials or $1/month for the first few months.

### Payment Processing Fees (Biggest Variable Cost)
Using **Shopify Payments** (recommended where possible):
- Basic: ~2.9% + $0.30 per online transaction.
- Grow: ~2.7% + $0.30.
- Advanced: ~2.5% + $0.30.

**For knives/swords/weapons**: Shopify Payments (powered by Stripe) often restricts or prohibits "weapons" categories. Many blade sellers need third-party gateways (e.g., Authorize.net or specialized knife-friendly processors), which adds Shopify's extra transaction fee (2% on Basic, 1% on Grow, 0.6% on Advanced) plus the gateway's own rates. This can increase effective fees noticeably.

On $10,000 monthly sales, expect **$300–$500+ in processing fees** alone, depending on plan and processor.

### Other Monthly/Yearly Costs
- **Domain**: $10–$20/year (~$1–2/month amortized). Buy via Shopify or external (e.g., Namecheap).
- **Theme**: Free basic options available; premium/custom $150–$350 one-time (or more for specialized designs).
- **Apps**: Common for inventory (large stock), shipping (especially heavy/swords/scopes), compliance, SEO, reviews, etc. Expect **$0–$100+ initially**, scaling to $100–$500+/month for advanced needs. Inventory/shipping apps often $20–$100 each.
- **Shipping**: Carrier rates + possible handling fees. Rural UP Michigan location may mean higher outbound costs or longer times. Discounts improve on higher plans.
- **Marketing/Ads**: Not Shopify-specific but essential — often the largest ongoing expense (Google/Facebook/SEO).
- **Other**: Email marketing, analytics, insurance, accounting tools, potential compliance tools for restricted items.

**Total realistic monthly cost for a starting knife store**:
- **Low end (Basic plan, low volume, minimal apps)**: $50–$150 (platform + basics).
- **Typical small/medium operation**: $150–$500+ (including apps, fees on sales, domain amortized).
- Scales with revenue due to transaction fees.

### Recommendations for Your Setup
With a large existing inventory, focus on good inventory management apps to avoid overselling. Test compliance/shipping rules early, as knives and scopes have restrictions by state and carrier. Consider a hybrid approach if doing local sales in the UP (tourism/hunting season).

Shopify is scalable and user-friendly for e-commerce, making it a strong choice, but budget for marketing and operations beyond the platform fee. Check Shopify's current pricing page for your location and test with their trial. For weapons-adjacent items, verify payment gateway options upfront to avoid account issues. Consult an accountant for tax implications in Michigan. Success depends more on products, marketing, and execution than the platform cost itself.
