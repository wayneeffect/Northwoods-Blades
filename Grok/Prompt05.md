### Hourly Rates for Mid-Level Shopify Developers (2026)
Mid-level (roughly 2–5+ years experience) Shopify-focused work typically falls in these ranges:
- **US / North America**: $55–$100/hr (averages often cited around $70–$85).
- **Global freelance platforms**: $40–$80/hr common for competent mid-level work.
- Using **AI tools** (e.g., for code generation, content, design assets, or rapid prototyping) boosts your efficiency, allowing you to deliver faster and justify rates toward the higher end of mid-level without increasing your time investment. Clients value speed and lower overall project cost.

**Position yourself at $65–$85/hr** if you're in the US (or serving US clients from the UP Michigan area) with a solid portfolio of completed stores, good communication, and AI-accelerated delivery. Lower ($50–$60) if competing broadly on platforms like Upwork; higher ($80–$100+) with niches like e-commerce for knives/outdoor gear, custom integrations, or performance optimization.

### Project-Based Pricing (Recommended for Most Store Builds)
Many clients prefer fixed quotes. For a typical knife store (large inventory upload, product variants, shipping rules for heavy/bladed items, basic compliance notes, payment setup, SEO basics, and theme customization):
- **Basic setup** (premium theme install/customization, product import, essentials): **$1,500–$4,000**.
- **Mid-level professional store** (custom sections, app integrations for inventory/shipping/reviews, branding, performance tweaks, launch support): **$3,000–$7,500** (most common range for mid-level developers).
- **Advanced** (heavy custom Liquid code, complex scopes/knives filtering, migrations, advanced SEO/content): **$6,000–$12,000+**.

**Your AI advantage**: You can often complete what used to take 40–60 hours in 20–40 hours, improving margins while offering competitive fixed prices or faster turnaround.

### Separate Website (If Needed)
Shopify includes built-in blogging, pages, and basic content features — a separate standalone website is usually **not required** for an online knife store. You can handle content marketing directly on Shopify.

- If the client wants a separate informational/marketing site (e.g., WordPress for heavy blogging/SEO): Add **$1,500–$4,000** (or $60–$90/hr) depending on complexity.
- Many developers bundle a basic Shopify build + minor separate landing pages or recommend keeping everything in one Shopify store for better SEO and conversions.

### Factors That Influence What You Can Charge
- **Scope** — Inventory size (large = more time for organization/variants), custom features (e.g., rifle scope filters, shipping restrictions for swords), integrations.
- **Your value-add** — AI efficiency, prior e-commerce experience, outdoor/gear niche knowledge, post-launch support/maintenance package (e.g., monthly retainer $300–$1,000).
- **Client location & expectations** — US clients (like your area) pay premium vs. global.
- **Deliverables** — Include training, documentation, basic training on Shopify admin.
- **Revisions & support** — Clearly define in contract (e.g., 2 rounds of revisions included).

**Tips for Pricing & Selling Your Services**:
- Offer tiered packages (Basic / Professional / Premium) to make decisions easier.
- Use value-based elements: Emphasize how your build supports high AOV items like scopes/swords and handles regulations/shipping.
- Require 30–50% upfront, milestones for the rest.
- Factor in your location's lower cost of living for competitive yet profitable rates.
- Test prices on platforms like Upwork, Shopify Partner Directory, or local Michigan/UP networks.

**Bottom line**: Aim for **$3,000–$6,000 fixed** for a solid mid-level Shopify knife store build (or equivalent hourly). This is competitive yet profitable with AI tools helping you work efficiently. Track your actual hours on the first few projects and adjust. Build a strong portfolio showcasing similar stores to command the higher end of the range. Success comes from clear scoping, reliable delivery, and results (e.g., fast load times, good mobile UX for shopping).
